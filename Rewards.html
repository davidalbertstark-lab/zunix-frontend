How to Engage & Earn

Ways to Earn Zunix Points (ZP)

Earn points for every value-driven action. This includes showing up daily, contributing content, engaging in discussions, and sharing knowledge within the community.

Daily & Baseline

What Boosts Engagement Score

Engagement Milestones

Reach new levels by completing interaction goals such as comments, shares, meaningful replies. Your consistency over time will boost your profile's status.

Some actions give only points like daily logins. Others increase your engagement score like profile completion or active reading. Some do both if you show real consistency.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I maximize my Zunix Points (ZP) daily?

To maximize ZP daily, consistently log in, complete your profile, engage with posts (likes, comments, shares), attend events, and refer verified users. Focus on quality contributions and hitting monthly milestones for bonus multipliers.

2. What counts as a “quality” post or contribution?

Quality means your posts add real value. no spam, plagiarism, or irrelevant content. Original ideas, insightful commentary, and resourceful info that sparks genuine engagement are what earn points and visibility.

3. How does the referral system work, and what are its limits?

You earn ZP for every 5 verified users who join via your referral link. This ensures genuine growth and prevents abuse while rewarding active community builders.

4. Can I lose Zunix Points, and if so, how?

Yes. Points can be deducted for spam, violating community guidelines, or inactivity over extended periods. Keeping consistent, positive engagement safeguards your score.

5. How are Zunix Points used beyond rewards?

ZP isn’t just for rewards, users spend points to access premium learning tools, enter Zunix’s monthly payout program, and unlock exclusive features as they roll out. It’s how serious members stay ahead and earn more.
