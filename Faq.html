Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zunix?
Zunix is a student-powered platform built to support African students with access to tools, gigs, exposure, and a growing student economy.
Who can use Zunix?
The platform is open to students, creators, freelancers, innovators, and campus leaders across African tertiary institutions.
Is Zunix free to use?
Yes. Core features are free to access. Premium tools, promotional perks, or visibility boosts may require optional activation.
How do I sign up?
Fill out the onboarding form with valid student details. Access is granted instantly or reviewed based on region and activity.
What can users earn on Zunix?
Users can earn through product sales, freelance gigs, brand tasks, student campaigns, and monthly engagement rewards.
What is the Zunix Points (ZP) system?
Zunix Points (ZP) are earned for platform actions like posting, referrals, or completing tasks. Points unlock rankings, rewards, and potential cashouts.
What is the Campus Ambassador Program?
Known internally as Zunix Groundwalkers, this program empowers student leaders to promote campaigns, lead campus movements, and earn through verified missions.
Can users sell on Zunix?
Yes. Verified users can sell digital or physical products, services, and freelance gigs directly on the marketplace with no transaction fees.
Are brand partnerships supported?
Yes. Verified brands can launch student campaigns, hire influencers, or showcase services via Zunixâ€™s brand network.
Is there a creator or writer program?
Yes. Students can join to publish articles, manage fan pages, run digital trends, or create content. High performers may earn rewards or digital leadership roles.
How are student innovators supported?
Innovators can access resources, early-stage guidance, community support, and may be connected to brand sponsors or investor circles.
How are students ranked or rewarded?
Rankings are based on Zunix Points and engagement score. Top users receive cash, merch, platform features, and exclusive invites monthly.
Does Zunix operate only in Nigeria?
Zunix is currently active in Nigeria but designed to scale across African campuses. Expansion is ongoing.
How is user data protected?
All user data is handled with strict privacy protocols and never sold or shared without consent. Encrypted systems secure login and engagement data.
How can support be contacted?
Reach out via the contact page or email: support@zunix.com.
Can non-students join Zunix?
While optimized for students, certain programs allow participation by recent graduates, alumni, or youth involved in the student ecosystem.
